[ General Server Rules ]

1. Be Respectful: It would be best to respect all users, regardless of your liking. Treat others the way you want to be treated.

2. No Sexual Discrimination: Includes Race, Color, Gender, Sexual Orientation, or Harassment of any members Direct or Indirectly in DM's or MPG's Discord.

3. No Inappropriate Language: The use of profanity is strictly forbidden.

4. No Spamming: Do not send a lot of small messages right after each other.

5. No Pinging Staff: Do not ping staff members to get a quick response or ask unnecessary questions.

6. No Asking For Roles / Ranks: It is forbidden to ask for roles and ranks for Any Server.

7. No Pornographic / Adult / Other NSFW: This community server is not meant to share this kind of material.

8. No Offensive Names Profile Pictures: You will be asked to change your name or picture if the staff deems them inappropriate.

9. No Shady or Hidden Links: You are not allowed to send links to shady websites or hide links in any way.

10. No Direct or Indirect Threats: Threats to other users of DDoS, Death, DoX, Abuse, or Other malicious threats are prohibited.

Punishments: Regardless of Role, or Money Donated, Any Member Breaking any of these Rules Reserves the Right to be Muted / Kicked / Banned at the @Owner, @Admins Discretion. It is not allowed to join the server with an alternative/second account in an act to dodge your punishment. Your SteamID may also be Blacklisted across the Entire Cluster, Depending on the Severity of your Actions also be uploaded to Global Blacklist. Banlist


[ Admin Responsibilities ]

1. Discord name and In-Game name must be the same to avoid confusion.

2. Minimum age requirement is 18.

3. Must have functioning headset. (no echoes, screeching or static)

4. W eDo not interfere with tribe issues unless it is affecting the server.

5. Ingame Admin need to make sure that everyone that logs into the server feels welcome.

6. Know the rules of the Server.

7. They must also follow the rules and guidelines of the community.

8. Admins have admin commands to help players, to bring won items from giveaways or for special events

9. They are not to use their admin commands to spawn coins / dinos / items, etc for any player to "help" Doing so does not let the player learn how to play the game themselves. Will also look like player favoritism and cliques.

10. Admins should be the first go-to for any problems in game. If they cannot fix the issue, then they should ask for help from @Owner.

11. They should not spawn in their own dinos / coins / items or play in creative mode. Admin Abuse will not be tolerated and will be Demoted Promptly

12. It is the responsibility of the In-Game admin to let new players know about F1, how to use the F2 and F3 shop, how to get points for both shops. And to let new players know how to link game / discord.

13. Do not change the weather/time of day of server.
